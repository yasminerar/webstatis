About Us

Since 2023, Dhia's Patisserie has been striving to find the best flour to make out finest breads, the Artisan Bucheron and the Baguette Tradition every night, ready for you to enjoy in the morning. Our wild yeast is an 8-year long secret, fermenting naturally somewhere in Bali. Started by three Parisian cousins in 2023.

Based in Cipete’s most-visited spots, Dhia's Patisserie serves a tempting range of high-quality handmade French pastries, cakes, artisan bread and savouries in a relaxed, friendly environment. The bakery is ideal for breakfast and lunch gatherings, and we strive to serve a healthy and delicious menu whatever the time of day.

Viennoiserie

All our pastries, also known as Viennoiseries, are handmade fresh daily Our signature pastry is the iconic Dhia's croissant.

Pâtisserie

We make all of the French classics daily by hand, such as Brioche bread, pain au chocolat, and almond croissants, pépite de feuilleté, as well as pain au raisin and more.

Our values

We believe in using only high quality, fresh, all-natural ingredients in our products. We do not use preservatives, additives, artificial coloring.
